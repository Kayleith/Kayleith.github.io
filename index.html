A Little More About Me

My name is Brian Kang. I went to school for Physics. I was always interested in science and math, but I felt like something was missing in my life.

Having finished AP computer science at the age of 16, I knew that code would always be a big part of my life. Even though my road has had a few twists and turns, I’ve finally returned to my passion -- programming. My decision to repursue my passion and love for programming, which led me to New York City, learning Web Development at App Academy.

I have experience in Java, Ruby, Rails, Javascript, and Backbone. Currently, I work with Swift as a software engineering contractor for weCliq. I seek opportunities to build engaging user experiences and solve interesting problems. Hopefully, with you!
